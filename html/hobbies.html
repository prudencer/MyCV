Why I Love News and Documentaries

Staying informed and intellectually engaged has always been important to me. Watching news and documentaries refreshes my mind and helps me grow both personally and professionally. One way I nurture this curiosity is through watching news and documentaries. These aren't just passive pastimes; they're windows into the world tools for understanding science, history, current affairs, and technology through real-life storytelling. This exercise both refreshes my mind and helps to develop personally and career-wise. It is quite interesting to understand how the world operates, how countries interact, and most importantly, how the global economies progress, what in our daily lives affect the economy and having a sort of a window into the future i.e. with the current US tarrif war, I can anticipate trade to be affected and prices to rise, therefore I should start being vigilent with my finances before the worst is imminent.

Fascination with Science

Science documentaries allow me to explore the universe from black holes to genetic engineering. Platforms like CuriosityStream and BBC Earth offer engaging, fact-based content that ignites wonder and a hunger for discovery. It is amazing to see scientific discoveries, how they have been used to improve our daily lives.

Exploring the Past

History documentaries deepen my understanding of past civilizations, conflicts, and movements. They remind me how history repeats itself and how much we can learn from our predecessors. History is crucial in understanding the present day, why things unfold the ways they do now, and this understanding assists us in finding resolutions, for instance, tribal conflicts in Africa like the unrest currently going on in the Eastern DRC.

Staying Informed with Current Affairs

News keep me grounded in present realities. I follow global news networks to understand political shifts, economic trends, and social movements that shape our world. This awareness enhances my ability to engage in thoughtful discussions.

Learning Through Technology

Technology-focused documentaries help me keep up with innovations in AI, cybersecurity, renewable energy, and digital culture. They provide insights into how technology transforms societies—and how we can adapt.
